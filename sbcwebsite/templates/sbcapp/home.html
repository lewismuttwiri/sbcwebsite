About Us

SBC Kenya has been a key player in the Kenyan beverage
market,aiming to provide quality products at affordable prices.

in four pillars.

Driving Breakthroughs

Pioneering product, packaging and delivery innovations for convenience and circularity.

Investing in Communities

Empowering local livelihoods through skills training, scholarships and sustainable opportunity programs.

Grant-funded partnerships that uplift entrepreneurs, improve infrastructure and strengthen communities.

Science‑based targets to cut emissions, expand recycling and advance net‑zero ambitions.
