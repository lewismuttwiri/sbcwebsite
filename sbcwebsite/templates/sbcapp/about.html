About SBC Kenya Ltd

Quenching Thirst, Inspiring Lives Since 2013

Our Story

Under the warm Kenyan sun, laughter dances between bustling markets. Here, the clink of ice against glass bottles echoes stories shared, moments celebrated, and thirsts quenched. This vibrant tapestry is what inspires Beverage Company Kenya, a journey that began in 2013 with a simple dream: to bottle happiness.

We're more than just a beverage company; we're a passionate team dedicated to crafting delicious experiences. From the electrifying bite of Mountain Dew to the refreshing lightness of Seven Up, our diverse portfolio offers a taste for every occasion.

Whether you're celebrating a special moment with friends or seeking a simple afternoon pick-me-up, there's a Beverage Company Kenya drink waiting to elevate your experience.
