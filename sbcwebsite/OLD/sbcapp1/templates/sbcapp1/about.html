ABOUT SBC KENYA LTD

Quenching Thirst, Inspiring Lives!

Under the warm Kenyan sun, laughter dances between bustling markets. Here, the clink of ice against glass bottles echoes stories shared, moments celebrated, and thirsts quenched. This vibrant tapestry is what inspires Beverage Company Kenya, a journey that began in 2013 with a simple dream: to bottle happiness.

We're more than just a beverage company; we're a passionate team dedicated to crafting delicious experiences. From the electrifying bite of Mountain Dew to the refreshing lightness of Seven Up, our diverse portfolio offers a taste for every occasion. Whether you're celebrating a special moment with friends or seeking a simple afternoon pick-me-up, there's a Beverage Company Kenya drink waiting to elevate your experience.

But our story goes beyond fizzy refreshment. We are deeply committed to responsible business practices and community engagement. We strive to minimize our environmental impact through responsible sourcing and efficient production processes. We source ingredients sustainably whenever possible, supporting Kenyan farmers and empowering local communities.

SBC KENYA LTD Quality Guarantee.

At SBC KENYA LTD, "refreshment" begins with "assurance." We're committed to delivering iconic brands like Pepsi, Mirinda, Seven Up, and Tonic, but just as crucial as their deliciousness is their unwavering quality and safety. That's why we have a strict Quality Assurance Policy, ensuring every sip you enjoy meets the highest standards.

Our quality core values:

  1. Safety First: We comply with the strictest regulations, implementing rigorous measures to eliminate potential hazards and prioritize consumer well-being.
  2. Unwavering Standards: From ingredient selection to bottling, every stage undergoes meticulous testing to guarantee consistent quality and taste.
  3. Continuous Improvement: We strive for constant evolution, actively seeking and implementing enhancements to stay at the forefront of quality practices.
  4. Transparency and Trust: We believe in open communication, readily sharing relevant data with stakeholders to earn and maintain your trust.

Key pillars of our policy:

  1. Rigorous Quality Control: From raw materials to finished products, our state-of-the-art lab conducts comprehensive analyses, guaranteeing safety and adherence to regulations.
  2. Dedicated Quality Team: Our team proactively monitors every step, addressing any potential deviations through regular checks and data analysis.
  3. Thorough Inspections: Every bottle undergoes rigorous checks before leaving, ensuring it meets our aesthetic and quality requirements.
